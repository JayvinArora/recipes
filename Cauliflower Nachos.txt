INGREDIENTS
2 small heads (or 1 large head) cauliflower, chopped
3 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
1 tsp. kosher salt or Himalayan salt
1/2 tsp. cumin
1/2 tsp. paprika
1/4 tsp. chili powder
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1 c. Colby-Jack, shredded
¼ cup chopped orange bell pepper
Salsa, for serving OR (¼ cup chopped tomato, ¼ cup chopped red onion)
Guacamole, for serving
1 medium jalapeño seeds and veins removed, for serving
 2 Tbsp. finely chopped cilantro

DIRECTIONS
Preheat oven to 425º.
To a large baking sheet, add cauliflower. Drizzle with olive oil. Sprinkle all over with salt, cumin, paprika, and chili powder. Toss to coat. 
Roast until tender crisp and golden, 20 to 25 minutes.
Top with Colby-Jack cheese and return to oven until melted, 5 minutes more.
Garnish with your favorite salsa (tomato, onion), guacamole, bell pepper, jalapeños, avocado, and cilantro; serve immediately.
